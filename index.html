    Student Chatrooms

    One of the major problems that affects all of us is the shallow standards that have become so common amongst many teenagers and adults. Instead of focusing on the character and personality of other people we tend to notice and judge people based on superficial values. ConnectEd is introducing a solution. Chatrooms. You start by selecting a topic. Afterwards we will connect you with someone that has selected the same or a similar topic. Both of you start chatting about this topic anonymously. After chatting for 10 minutes you can choose whether you want to reveal your username or not. This service will help you create unexpected friendships with various people and these friendships will be true and lasting because they are based on common interests and values rather than shallow standards.

    About Us

    ConnectEd is a mobile application exclusively for students in international high schools across Egypt. The application aims to make the modern lives of Egyptian teenagers easier, by providing a variety of different services in the fields of education, culture and social life. The services provided introduce students with similar interests to each other, notify them of upcoming local events and help ease their anxieties before exams, among other things. The application will revolutionize the ways students use to improve academically and socially.

    Services

    One of the most challenging problems students face is their inability to understand in school. Since many students face the same problem experts conducted many studies and researches in order to find out the reasons behind it. This problem exists not because students don’t pay attention or because they’re not intelligent enough but because the teacher explains the topic in a more complicated manner for a teenager’s brain to understand. Based on many studies and researches a student is 72% more likely to understand a topic if it’s explained by someone his age rather by than a teacher or adult. ConnectEd’s first academical service is basically connecting Students, that are good at a certain subject with others who aren’t. Getting tutored by someone your age will not only cost less but it will also make you improve your performance at school and will open a way for you to make new friendships. As for the tutors this is an opportunity to start making money while learning and improving your teaching abilities. The best way to study is to explain what you’re trying to study to someone else!
